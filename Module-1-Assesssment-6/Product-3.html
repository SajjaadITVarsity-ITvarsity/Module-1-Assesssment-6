THREE MODES OF PLAY

- TV mode: Dock your Nintendo Switch to enjoy HD gaming on your TV, either solo or with family and friends.

- Tabletop mode: Flip the stand to share the screen and the Joy-Con to share the fun with a multiplayer game.

- Handheld mode: Attach the Joy-Con controllers and play your Switch on the go.

SHARE THE FUN WITH JOY-CON CONTROLLERS

Nintendo Switch has a controller on each side of the console that function together, called the Joy-Con. These Joy-Con controllers feature motion control and HD rumble which puts you right in the game. If you attach the two Joy-Con to the Joy-Con grip, it will work like a traditional controller, and without the grip they work as two individual, fully-functioning controllers. Hand one of the Joy-Con controllers to a friend for multiplayer fun.

BETTER TOGETHER: MULTIPLAYER GAMING OPTIONS

- Local co-op: Play on the big screen at home with friends.*

- Local wireless: Link up to eight Nintendo Switch and Nintendo Switch Lite systems together to play multiplayer games anywhere.

- Online play: Team up or face off with others online** in compatible games with a Nintendo Switch Online membership.

ENHANCE YOUR GAMEPLAY WITH AMIIBO

Amiibo are a fun and unique way to interact with your favourite Nintendo characters and games! Tap an amiibo against the right Joy-Con to acquire special characters, unlock new content, access new missions, and more!
